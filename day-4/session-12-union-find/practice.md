## Learning Path

**Recommended order:**
1. Start with 1-3 (basic template practice)
2. Move to 4-5 (cycle detection and grouping)
3. Try 6-7 (optimization problems)
4. Tackle 8-9 (special applications)
5. Finish with 10 (advanced weighted UF)
